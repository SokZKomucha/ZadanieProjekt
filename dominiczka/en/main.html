What is the process of fermentation?

Fermentation is the process of organic substance transformation by microorganisms, such as bacteria or yeast, in anaerobic conditions. During fermentation, organic substances are broken down, and various byproducts are produced.

What are the different types of fermentation?

There are three main types of fermentation: alcohol fermentation, acetic fermentation, and lactic fermentation. They differ in substrates and products, but all of them work in similar way
